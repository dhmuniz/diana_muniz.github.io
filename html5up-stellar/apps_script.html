Objective

Implement a streamlined process for managing expired leads, reducing manual effort and improving efficiency in lead tracking and agent performance metrics.

Challenge

The existing process for managing expired insurance enrollment leads was labor-intensive, leading to mismanagement and inefficiencies. Managers had to manually identify responsible agents for expired leads and communicate across teams, which was not only time-consuming but also resulted in missed opportunities for timely follow-ups and accurate data tracking.

Innovation

Developed an intuitive automation system leveraging Google Forms, Google Sheets, and Google Apps Script to optimize the expired lead management process. This solution minimized data entry requirements and automated the communication loop between managers and agents.

Process

Upon form submission by a manager which required minimal information, the system utilized a Google Sheet to pull in the information needed to notify the appropriate manager. A Google Apps Script, Triggered by the form submission, formatted the email content, embedded with the specific lead ID, and linked a procedural guide, then automatically dispatched an email to the agent with the relevant information and action steps, and CC'd the manager for visibility.

Technology Stack

  • Google Forms for user input
  • Google Sheets for data management and look up funcions
  • Google Apps Script for automation and email triggers
  • JavaScript (underlying language of Google Apps Script)

Outcomes

This automated system led to a 75% reduction in expired leads, as the process adherence by managers improved significantly. The real-time graph on the Google Sheet allowed for the prediction of peak times for expired leads, enabling proactive measures. Customized reports generated by the system provided insights into agent performance, facilitating targeted coaching and improved adherence to lead management protocols.
