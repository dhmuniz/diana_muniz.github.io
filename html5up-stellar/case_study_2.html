Streamlining Reporting Processes

Challenge

Managers were spending excessive time pulling reporting to monitor their agents' real-time KPI throughout the day. The process was extremely inefficient, and with so many people pulling the same BI reports continuously it created system strain, which led reporting to crash regularly. This caused delays in visibility, and more work for our BI partners.

Role and Goals

My objective was to design a solution that would consolidate report generation, reducing the time managers spent on this task, and improve system performance.

Solution

I created a regional Google Sheet that allowed managers to pull data from a single source. This approach centralized the data, making it more accessible and reducing the burden on our systems.

Outcome and Results

The new system significantly reduced the time managers spent on report generation, provided more frequent updates to agents, and alleviated system crashes due to high traffic. This innovation not only saved time but also improved the overall efficiency of the team.
